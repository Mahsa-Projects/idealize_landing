How It Works



  • Create Project

    You can create a project to align with your interests and capabilities. The project can be either a new idea you come up with or an existing project that you want to share with the community. You can also add a description to the project to give more information about it.

  • Project List

    Browse through a curated list of projects with different tags and course of studies. The mentioned list is generated based on each user's preferences, in the future we will personalized recommendations tailored to your unique profile.

  • Project Details

    Gain a deeper understanding of a project by exploring its details, including project requirements, objectives, and desired outcomes. You can also see which skills are needed for the project and how you can contribute.

  • Draft Project

    Collaborate with project partners by sharing notes, files, and ideas in our collaborative workspace. This allows you to work efficiently with your team, even if you are not in the same location. Our collaborative workspace also allows you to assign tasks to each other, set deadlines, and track progress.

  • Profile Details

    Customize your profile to showcase your skills, interests, and experience. Add a profile picture, tags, relevant skills and interests to showcase your strengths and areas of expertise. This will help you to get discovered by other users looking for partners for their projects.

    • Future Features

  • Matchmaking (Coming Soon)

    Our matchmaking feature will connect you with other users who share similar interests and skills, making it easier for you to find project partners and collaborate effectively.

  • AI Chat Bot (Coming Soon)

    An AI-powered chat bot will assist you throughout your project journey, offering instant support and guidance. It will be available 24/7 to answer your questions, provide recommendations, and help you navigate through various project phases with ease.
