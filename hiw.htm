How It Works



  • Discover and Explore Interdisciplinary Projects

    Dive into a vibrant world of ideas! Browse through a list of interdisciplinary projects tagged by interests and fields of study. The list is tailored to your preferences – and soon, it will be even smarter, with AI-powered recommendations just for you!

  • Project Details – View and Engage

    Curious about a project? Click in to see all the details – from objectives and requirements to desired outcomes. Discover what skills are needed and how you can contribute. Get inspired and find your place in something exciting.

  • Create a Project – Share Your Vision

    Have an idea? Bring it to life! Whether it’s a fresh concept or an existing initiative from a study course or research project you want to share, creating a project is easy and intuitive. Add a description, set your goals, and invite others to join your journey.

  • Draft Project – Co-Create Before You Publish

    Got an idea in progress? With the draft feature, you and your project partners can collaborate behind the scenes before going public. Edit the project together, refine your descriptions, and align on goals – and once it’s ready, publish it with confidence. Perfect for teams who want to polish their concept before sharing it with the world!

  • Profile Details – Show Who You Are

    Make your profile shine! Add a picture, your skills, interests, and experience. The more you personalize, the easier it is for others to find and connect with you. Show the community what you bring to the table.

  • Notifications – Stay in the Loop

    Never miss a beat! Get real-time updates on your projects, from new comments and updates to invitations to collaborate. You’re always informed, always connected.

    • Coming Soon: Future Features

  • AI Chat Bot

    Say hello to your new digital sidekick! Our smart chatbot will guide you to projects that match your interests, answer questions, and support your journey every step of the way.

  • Matchmaking

    Find your perfect match! Soon, our system will suggest partners with shared interests and complementary skills, so you can build your dream team in no time.
